# Purpose

This document defines the legal principles governing TradePilot AI.

Its purpose is to establish clear expectations for users, define the application's responsibilities, and reduce legal exposure.

---

# Product Classification

TradePilot AI is an investment decision support platform.

TradePilot AI is **not**:

- A broker
- A financial advisor
- A portfolio manager
- An investment manager
- An automated trading system

The application assists users by presenting statistical analysis and historical evidence.

---

# No Financial Advice

TradePilot AI does not provide financial advice.

All recommendations are informational and educational.

Recommendations shall never be interpreted as investment advice.

---

# User Responsibility

Users remain solely responsible for:

- Investment decisions
- Buy decisions
- Sell decisions
- Portfolio management
- Risk management

The application does not execute trades on behalf of users.

---

# Recommendation Disclaimer

Every recommendation generated by TradePilot AI represents an evaluation of available information at a specific point in time.

Recommendations are based on:

- Statistical analysis
- Historical evidence
- Market information

Future market behavior cannot be guaranteed.

---

# No Guarantee

TradePilot AI makes no guarantees regarding:

- Investment performance
- Market direction
- Profitability
- Loss prevention

Past performance does not guarantee future results.

---

# AI Transparency

TradePilot AI explains the reasoning behind its recommendations whenever possible.

Transparency does not imply certainty.

AI output represents probability-based analysis rather than factual prediction.

---

# Market Data

TradePilot AI relies on external market data providers.

Temporary interruptions, delays, or inaccuracies in external data may affect recommendations.

The application cannot guarantee uninterrupted availability of third-party services.

---

# Limitation of Liability

To the maximum extent permitted by applicable law:

TradePilot AI shall not be liable for:

- Investment losses
- Lost profits
- Indirect damages
- Consequential damages
- Trading decisions made by users

---

# Intellectual Property

All source code, AI logic, documentation, algorithms, branding, and visual assets are the intellectual property of TradePilot AI unless otherwise stated.

Unauthorized copying, distribution, or reverse engineering is prohibited where permitted by law.

---

# Privacy

User information shall be handled in accordance with the project's Privacy Policy.

Only information necessary for providing the service shall be collected.

---

# Future Terms

Prior to public release, TradePilot AI shall publish:

- Terms of Service
- Privacy Policy
- Cookie Policy (if applicable)

These documents shall complement, but not replace, this Legal Specification.

---

# Legal Principles

TradePilot AI is committed to:

- Transparency
- Honesty
- Responsible AI usage
- Respect for user autonomy
- Compliance with applicable laws and regulations
